

quoter and swap
----------------------------

some simple examples of quoter and swap

in this tutorial, we will do quoter and swap with exact input amount or exact output amount in iZiSwap.

here, **quoter** means pre-querying amount of token acquired or token needed to pay

**swap** means doing real swap with given exact paying amount or exact acquiring amount

in this example, quoter and swap are called throw 2 different contracts.
